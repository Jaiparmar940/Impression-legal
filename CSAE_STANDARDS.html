Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: 07/14/2025

At Impression, we are committed to creating a safe environment for all users and have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This document outlines our comprehensive approach to preventing, detecting, and reporting CSAE content on our platform.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Impression maintains a zero tolerance policy for any content involving child sexual abuse and exploitation. This includes:

  • Images or videos depicting minors in sexual contexts
  • Content that sexualizes minors
  • Grooming behavior or attempts to exploit minors
  • Any form of child pornography or child sexual abuse material
  • Content that promotes or facilitates CSAE

Any violation of this policy will result in immediate account termination and reporting to appropriate authorities.

2. Age Verification and Protection

We implement multiple layers of protection to prevent minors from accessing inappropriate content:

  • Age Verification: Users must be 18+ to create an account
  • Content Moderation: All user-generated content is reviewed for age-appropriate content
  • AI Detection: Automated systems scan for potential CSAE content
  • Human Review: Trained moderators review flagged content

3. Content Reporting System

How to Report CSAE Content:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the "Report" button on any profile or content
  • Email Reporting: Send detailed reports to safety@impression.app
  • Emergency Contact: For immediate concerns, contact us at +1-800-XXX-XXXX

What to Include in Your Report:

  • Description of the concerning content
  • Username or profile information
  • Screenshots or evidence (if safe to capture)
  • Date and time of the incident
  • Any additional context that may be helpful

4. Automated Detection Systems

Our platform employs advanced technology to detect potential CSAE content:

  • Image Analysis: AI-powered systems scan uploaded photos for inappropriate content
  • Text Analysis: Natural language processing detects concerning language patterns
  • Behavioral Analysis: Systems monitor user behavior for suspicious patterns
  • Cross-Platform Intelligence: We collaborate with industry partners to identify known offenders

5. Law Enforcement Cooperation

We maintain strong relationships with law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations:

  • Immediate reporting of suspected CSAE to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Cooperation with international law enforcement agencies
  • Preservation of evidence and user data when required by law
  • Participation in industry-wide initiatives to combat CSAE

6. User Education and Prevention

We believe education is key to prevention:

  • Safety Guidelines: Clear guidelines on appropriate content and behavior
  • Reporting Tools: Easy-to-use reporting mechanisms throughout the app
  • Community Guidelines: Regular reminders about our safety policies
  • Parental Resources: Information for parents about online safety

7. Content Moderation Process

Our content moderation follows a rigorous process:

  1. Automated Screening: All content is scanned by AI systems
  2. Human Review: Flagged content is reviewed by trained moderators
  3. Expert Assessment: Complex cases are escalated to specialized teams
  4. Law Enforcement: Confirmed CSAE content is immediately reported
  5. Account Action: Violating accounts are permanently banned

8. Data Retention and Privacy

We balance privacy with safety requirements:

  • User data is retained only as necessary for safety and legal compliance
  • CSAE-related data may be retained longer for law enforcement purposes
  • We comply with all applicable privacy laws while maintaining safety standards
  • Users can request data deletion, except where retention is legally required

9. Industry Collaboration

We actively participate in industry efforts to combat CSAE:

  • Membership in the Technology Coalition
  • Participation in the WeProtect Global Alliance
  • Collaboration with other dating and social platforms
  • Sharing of best practices and threat intelligence

10. Continuous Improvement

Our safety systems are constantly evolving:

  • Regular updates to detection algorithms
  • Ongoing training for moderation teams
  • User feedback integration
  • Industry best practice adoption
  • Regular safety audits and assessments
