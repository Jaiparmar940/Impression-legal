Data Deletion Instructions

At Impression, we respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal data. This guide explains how to delete your account and all associated data from our platform.

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once you delete your account, all of your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

Open the Impression App

Launch the Impression app on your device and ensure you're logged into your account.

Navigate to Profile Settings

Go to the Profile tab and tap on "Edit Profile" to access your account settings.

Find the Delete Account Section

Scroll down to the "Account Management" section and locate the "Delete Account" button.

Confirm Deletion

Tap the "Delete Account" button. You'll be presented with a detailed confirmation dialog explaining what will be deleted.

Final Confirmation

Review the information carefully and tap "Delete Account" in the final confirmation dialog to permanently delete your account.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your email, display name, age, gender, and account settings
  • Profile Variations: All profile variations you've created, including photos and prompts
  • Reviews: All reviews you've submitted for other users' profiles
  • Profile Reviews: All reviews submitted by other users for your profiles
  • Reports: All reports you've submitted and reports submitted about your content
  • Token Balance: Any remaining tokens in your account
  • Activity Data: All interaction history and usage data

Partial Data Deletion

If you need to delete specific data rather than your entire account, please contact our support team directly. We can help you with:

  • Deleting specific profile variations
  • Removing individual photos or prompts
  • Deleting specific reviews you've submitted
  • Updating or correcting personal information
  • Other data modification requests

Data Retention

We may retain certain information for up to 30 days after account deletion in case you want to reactivate your account. After this period, all data is permanently removed from our systems.

Privacy Commitment

At Impression, we are committed to protecting your privacy and giving you full control over your personal data. Our data deletion process is designed to be transparent and comprehensive, ensuring that your information is completely removed from our platform when you choose to delete your account.
